📖 My Daily Routine

Anna wakes up at 7 AM every day. She brushes her teeth and washes her face. Then she goes to the kitchen and eats breakfast. She usually has cereal with milk and a banana.

After breakfast, Anna gets dressed for school. She puts on her uniform and packs her bag. She leaves home at 8:15 AM and walks to school. The school is near her house, so it only takes 10 minutes.

At school, Anna has classes from 9 AM to 3 PM. She likes English and math the most. She eats lunch with her friends at 12 PM in the cafeteria.

After school, Anna goes home and does her homework. Then she plays with her little brother for an hour. At 7 PM, the family has dinner together. Anna goes to bed at 9 PM.

📝 Comprehension Questions
1. What time does Anna wake up?
2. What does Anna have for breakfast?
3. How does Anna get to school?
4. What subjects does Anna like most?
5. What time does Anna go to bed?
