📚 ENGLISH GRAMMAR

⏰ TENSES

• Present Simple
📌 Use: Facts, habits, routines
💬 Example: "She works in an office"
• Past Simple
📌 Use: Completed actions in past
💬 Example: "They went to London"
• Future Simple
📌 Use: Predictions, promises
💬 Example: "I will call you"

📍 PREPOSITIONS

Time (at, on, in)
Place (at, on, in)
Movement (to, into, onto)
